Identity Roles

Design the company and the AI as one system with two distinct jobs.

Anarix

The marketplace intelligence body.

Use Anarix when speaking about the company, the operating system, the analytical foundation, and the product surface that helps brands grow profitably across Amazon and Walmart.

  • Represents structure, reliability, and the operating layer.
  • Leads dashboards, settings, reporting, and system trust.
  • Holds the visual discipline of the product.
  • Stays calm, measured, and infrastructure-first.

Aan

The coral intelligence that lives above action.

Aan inherits the diamond from the Anarix symbol and turns it into a distinct AI presence. If Anarix is the body, Aan is the brain connected to every node, every screen, and every decision path.

  • Appears in AI entry points, analysis moments, and guided action flows.
  • Uses coral as its signature color, never as a general product accent.
  • Responds to the cursor with awareness, not playfulness.
  • Morphs when processing, then resolves into clarity.

Philosophy

Analytics + metrics build Anarix. Meaning, dignity, and action give Aan depth.

Analytics + Metrics = Anarix

The parent brand should feel exact, operational, and durable. It is the system that makes complexity legible and turns marketplace data into decisions.

Aan is the coral diamond docking on top of those two pillars. Read literally, it is the node sitting above analysis and metrics. Read behaviorally, it is the AI that stays on top of every action.

Aan as a name

Self-respect, esteem, grace, peace, and the sun.

  • Dignity and self-respect reinforce trust and composure.
  • The sun suggests guidance, visibility, and being above the horizon line.
  • Grace and peace keep the AI from feeling aggressive or noisy.
  • Wisdom and analysis connect naturally with Anarix's analytical core.

The two lower pillars represent analysis and metrics holding the system upright.

The coral diamond is Aan: the node, the brain, the intelligence above action.

Together they imply a connected body where every part can be understood and acted on.

Logo Usage

Dark and light usage rules for both Anarix and Aan.

Parent Brand

Anarix logo usage

Use Anarix for company communications, platform-level surfaces, product chrome, and non-AI system touchpoints.

Anarix on light

Preferred for warm paper, decks, documentation, and light landing sections.

Anarix on dark

Use on midnight, deep navy, or dark application surfaces where the wordmark must stay crisp and calm.

AI Layer

Aan by Anarix logo usage

Use Aan by Anarix for AI entry points, guided analysis, action surfaces, and brand moments where the coral intelligence should be explicitly named.

Aan on light

Use when the AI is present but the surrounding surface remains editorial or light.

Aan on dark

Preferred for AI workspace headers, floating action islands, and command surfaces.

Clear space

Keep at least one diamond width of breathing room around every approved lockup.

Color authority

Coral is reserved for Aan and AI emphasis. Do not turn it into a general CTA color.

Background pairing

Choose the dark or light version based on contrast first, never based on mood alone.

Compact usage

Use symbol-only marks for micro UI, AI launchers, tabs, and compact navigation.

System Rules

Keep the product disciplined. Let Aan add intelligence, not chaos.

Color Governance

Preserve the core Anarix product palette.

Periwinkle product base

Use the established light and dark product palette for system trust.

Blue as structure

Use blue for product actions, navigation, links, and parent-brand stability.

Coral as Aan-only accent

Reserve coral for AI launchers, intelligence feedback, and mascot moments.

Gradient as AI surface only

Use the coral-to-blue gradient inside Aan environments, never across routine analytics UI.

Typography

Let Anarix stay exact. Let Aan feel human.

Display

Editorial clarity for brand statements.

Interface

Operational language for product labels and UI.

Aan accent

Aan

  • Keep body copy active, short, and exact.
  • Use decorative script only for Aan signatures and intentional brand moments.
  • Never let expressive type bleed into dense data surfaces.

Aan Motion

Aan should feel aware, intelligent, and responsive without turning into a toy.

Idle

Aan waits above the system, calm and ready.

Interaction States

Idle is a suspended, balanced state. Aan should look like it is aware of the system even when it is not actively doing anything.

  • Use a subtle float, not a bounce.
  • Keep the diamond above the pillars even when the state changes.
  • Make presence legible before adding flourish.

Voice and Conduct

Sound like a capable guide, not a noisy assistant.

Anarix voice

Composed, exact, operational.

Use Anarix voice for system orientation, reporting, configuration, and platform-level communication. It should feel stable, clear, and quietly authoritative.

Aan voice

Warm, observant, and action-ready.

Aan should guide with respect. It can feel intelligent and human, but it should never sound overconfident, theatrical, or casual about real decisions.

Do say

I found three profitable actions to review. Start with the one that protects margin.

Avoid

I totally fixed everything for you. This one is a no-brainer, just do it.
